Key Club

Key Club is the largest nation-wide service program for high school students aimed at encouraging leadership through serving others and the community. Our goal is to carry out service in their communities, such as cleaning up parks, collecting clothing and organizing food drives. We aim to learn valuable leadership skills by running meetings and planning projects that will be hosted throughout the school year.

Mission Statement

BCA Key Club is a student-led organization that provides its members with opportunities to provide service, build character and develop leadership.

Core Values

Compassion: Compassion towards one another and one's community is integral to one's service to society.

Character: Character is built gradually everyday through acts of kindness and helpfulness through teamwork and dedication.

Leadership: A true leader listens and acts for the service and benefit of his/her people.

Inclusiveness: We push for equality, and welcome people of all backgrounds and ethnicities to make a difference in our society!
